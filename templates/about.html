About Us

Making healthcare navigation simple with MapmyIndia technology

What is AI Doctor Analysis?

AI Doctor Analysis is an innovative healthcare navigation platform that helps you find the right hospital quickly using India's best mapping technology - MapmyIndia (Mappls).

Our interactive body map lets you simply click where you feel pain, and we instantly show you nearby hospitals specializing in that area.

🇮🇳 Powered by MapmyIndia

We use MapmyIndia (Mappls) - India's most accurate and comprehensive mapping platform. Benefits:

  • Best coverage of Indian hospitals and clinics
  • Accurate addresses in local languages
  • Government-approved mapping data
  • Updated hospital information
  • Made in India, for India

Our Mission

To make healthcare accessible by providing fast, accurate, location-based hospital recommendations for every Indian.
